How Hostel Stuff works

Hostel Stuff helps students buy and sell used campus items directly. Listings, messages, favorites, and owner controls are kept simple so students can make quick local deals.

List an item

Add the item name, price, condition, college, contact details, and a real photo so buyers can judge the item clearly.

Search and compare

Students can browse by category, search for items, filter by college, save favorites, and open details before contacting the seller.

Connect safely

Interested students contact the owner, agree on pickup, inspect the item, and complete the exchange directly on campus.

Why is the Owner PIN or password needed?

The Owner PIN proves that you are the person who created the listing. It is used only for owner actions like marking an item as sold or deleting the listing later.

Is the Owner PIN my login password?

No. It is not a login password and should not be the same as your email, banking, college, or social media password. Use a simple 4-digit code only for this listing.

Can other students see my PIN?

No PIN is shown on the listing page. Still, keep it private because anyone with the PIN can manage that listing.

Why do I need to add a real item photo?

A real photo builds trust and helps buyers see the current condition of the item before they message you.

Why do sellers add phone or email?

Contact details help students arrange pickup quickly. Buyers should use them only for the listed item and avoid sharing unnecessary personal information.

How long does a listing stay active?

Active listings automatically expire after 14 days so old or unavailable items do not stay in the main catalog forever.

What should students check before buying?

Check the item photo, ask about defects, test electronics before paying, and meet in a public campus area.
